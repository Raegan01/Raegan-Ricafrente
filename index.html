Hello, I’m Raegan

I’m a Graphic Designer and Web Designer with 2+ years of experience creating polished, brand-consistent visuals for online marketing.

I help businesses design product ads, social media creatives, posters, promotional graphics, landing page layouts, website visuals, and A/B ad variations that look clean, professional, and aligned with their brand.

My work focuses on clear visual hierarchy, spacing, typography, layout balance, brand consistency, and call-to-action placement, so every design feels organized, purposeful, and ready for real marketing use.

Problems I Help Solve

Inconsistent brand visuals?

I create clean, brand-consistent designs that make your business look polished across ads, social media, and web platforms.

Weak visual hierarchy?

I organize layouts with clear spacing, typography, and structure so your message is easier to read and understand.

Cluttered marketing designs?

I simplify the layout and guide attention toward the most important message, product, or call-to-action.

Unpolished product presentation?

I design product ads, posters, and promotional graphics that make your offer look more professional and market-ready.

Off-brand design direction?

I align every visual with your brand style, tone, colors, and marketing goals.

Need for ad variations?

I create A/B design variations that help you test different creative angles while keeping the brand consistent.
